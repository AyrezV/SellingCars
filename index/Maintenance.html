CALENDAR MAINTENANCE

WAM Car pays great attention to these tests because these are the factors that greatly affect the vehicle's storage and durability.
In the warranty book for customers, there are nine (09) periodic maintenance tickets to use this service.

During the first 4 inspections, the owner only has to pay for the used materials (engine oil, water refill, battery charger, natural wear parts), and for repairs that are not included. Included in the maintenance schedule (see User Manual for each vehicle)

Periodic inspection and maintenance of Yamaha motorcycles must be carried out according to the maintenance schedule in the User's Manual and must be performed by the authorized Service Shop or Station for this warranty to be valid. The periodic maintenance inspection is determined by the number of km or month of use, whichever comes first (see Warranty card)
Before running away, in addition to regular inspection, special inspection at the factory is required (at your own expense).

In addition, before driving, check the following points: horn, light, steering wheel, turn signal (signal), brake (brake), clutch (ambraya), tire (cover), rearview mirror, level. gasoline and motor oil.
